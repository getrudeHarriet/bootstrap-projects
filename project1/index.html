What is Cameo

Cameo is an innovative camera app for both android and IOS that lets you capture the best moments in life either on still photos or video. Cameo has many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editting tools, filters and effects. And when you are done you can easily share with your friends and the rest of the world through instagram.

Cameo has been designed from the bottom up by photography experts and award-winning app developers to make sure you get the best camera app ever created. We are continously improving the app with new features - this is just the beginning.
